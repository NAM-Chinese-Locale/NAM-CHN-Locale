JENXPARIS Pont Notre Dame. The Pont Notre-Dame is a bridge that crosses the Seine in Paris, France linking the quai de Gesvres on the Rive Droite with the quai de la Corse on the Île de la Cité. The bridge is noted for being the "most ancient" in Paris, in the sense that, while the oldest bridge in Paris that is in its original state is undoubtedly the Pont Neuf, a bridge in some form has existed at the site of the Pont Notre-Dame since antiquity.

圣母桥，由xannapan制作。圣母桥是法国巴黎一座横跨于塞纳河之上的桥梁（部分地名暂未有中文翻译）。这座桥以“巴黎最古老的桥”而闻名。虽然按“原样保留下来最古老的桥梁”这个标准进行评判，巴黎最古老的桥无疑是新桥，但是如果按照“以某种形式在原地存续”这一标准来评定的话，那么“圣母桥是巴黎最古老的桥”这个观点也未尝是错的。
