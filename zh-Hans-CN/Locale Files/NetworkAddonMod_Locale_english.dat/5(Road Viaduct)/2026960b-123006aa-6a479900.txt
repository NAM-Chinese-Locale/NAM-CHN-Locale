For building Avenue Puzzle Pieces that interface with the RHW
Button contains pieces to interface between the NAM Elevated Avenue Puzzle Pieces and RHW networks, including overpasses, MIS ramp interfaces, and intersections
Use the Home/End key to rotate the Puzzle Piece & use the TAB key to cycle through the different types of Avenue/RHW Pieces
When the cursor changes from red to green, click to place.
FlexSPUI and Diverging Diamond pieces are found under this button
----------
此按钮用于建设大道高架桥与真实高速公路平立交与路口拼块，包含NAM大道15米高架桥拼块和RHW的交接，包括立交、大道的MIS匝道口以及交叉口。
按Home/End键旋转拼块，按Tab键切换不同拼块。
当光标从红色变为绿色时，单击以建造拼块。
单点菱形立交和分流菱形立交拼块可在选项的末端找到。
