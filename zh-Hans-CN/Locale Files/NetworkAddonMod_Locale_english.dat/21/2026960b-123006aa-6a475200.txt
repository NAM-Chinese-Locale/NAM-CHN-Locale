For building RealHighway Wide Curve Pieces
Button contains pieces to build Wide Curves for the RHW networks
Use the Home/End key to rotate the Puzzle Piece & use the TAB key to cycle through the different types of Curve Pieces
When the cursor changes from red to green, click to place.

按钮包含了RHW的各种弯道拼块。
使用 Home/End 键来旋转拼块 & 使用TAB键来切换不同的RHW弯道拼块。
当徽标从红色变为绿色时，点击以放置。
