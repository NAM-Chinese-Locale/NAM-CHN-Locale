For building Draggable Elevated Road Viaducts
Button contains several different Starter and FLEX Pieces for Draggable Elevated Road Viaducts
Use the Home/End key to rotate the Puzzle Piece & use the TAB key to cycle through the different types of Draggable Elevated Road Viaduct pieces
When the cursor changes from red to green, click to place

使用Home与End键来旋转拼块，使用Tab键在不同的拖拽道路高架之间循环切换
当光标由红色变为绿色时，点击左键来放置
